About Hypnic

Built for a South Africa that runs on better data and bigger experiences.

Hypnic is a privately held South African technology company. We operate two deliberately sequenced divisions — data services that generate value today, and immersive technology that defines tomorrow.

We believe the gap between large corporates and small businesses — in data infrastructure, in digital experience, in access to technology — is not inevitable. It is solvable.

Our Philosophy
